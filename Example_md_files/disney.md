# 🏰 The History of Disney

## Introduction

Disney is one of the world's most influential entertainment companies. From a small animation studio in the 1920s to a global media empire, Disney has shaped generations through animated films, television, theme parks, and streaming services.

![Disney Castle](https://i.pinimg.com/1200x/7e/01/a9/7e01a9cd9334f303d69b27cbffe31cd0.jpg)

---

# 👨 Walt Disney: The Founder

**Walter Elias Disney** was born on December 5, 1901, in Chicago, Illinois.

He developed an interest in drawing and storytelling at an early age. Along with his brother **Roy O. Disney**, he founded what would become The Walt Disney Company.

| Founder       | Birth Year | Role                            |
| ------------- | ---------- | ------------------------------- |
| Walt Disney   | 1901       | Creative visionary and animator |
| Roy O. Disney | 1893       | Business manager and co-founder |

---

# 🎬 The Beginning (1923)

In 1923, Walt and Roy established the **Disney Brothers Studio** in Hollywood, California.

### Early Milestones

* 1923: Disney Brothers Studio founded
* 1927: Creation of Oswald the Lucky Rabbit
* 1928: Creation of Mickey Mouse
* 1928: Release of *Steamboat Willie*

---

# 🐭 The Birth of Mickey Mouse

After losing the rights to Oswald the Lucky Rabbit, Walt Disney created a new character:

## Mickey Mouse

Mickey Mouse first appeared in **Steamboat Willie** (1928), one of the first cartoons with synchronized sound.

![Mickey Mouse](https://i.pinimg.com/1200x/cd/c0/7c/cdc07c51b3140204672112a784b21704.jpg)

### Why Mickey Was Important

* Became Disney's mascot
* Increased studio popularity
* Revolutionized animated storytelling

---

# 🎨 The Golden Age of Animation

Disney continued to innovate throughout the 1930s and 1940s.

## Major Animated Films

| Year | Film                            |
| ---- | ------------------------------- |
| 1937 | Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs |
| 1940 | Pinocchio                       |
| 1940 | Fantasia                        |
| 1941 | Dumbo                           |
| 1942 | Bambi                           |

### Snow White's Success

*Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs* was the world's first full-length animated feature film and became a major commercial success.

---

# 🌍 Expansion Beyond Movies

Disney expanded into television, merchandise, and live entertainment.

### Key Developments

* Television programs in the 1950s
* Character merchandise
* International audience growth
* New animation technologies

---

# 🎢 Disneyland Opens (1955)

One of Walt Disney's biggest dreams was creating a family-friendly theme park.

## Disneyland

Opened on July 17, 1955, in Anaheim, California.

### Attractions Included

* Main Street, U.S.A.
* Adventureland
* Frontierland
* Fantasyland
* Tomorrowland

![Disneyland](https://i.pinimg.com/736x/fa/79/1b/fa791bea1646449a9f6f6584c1a52861.jpg)

---

# ⭐ The Walt Disney World Era

After Walt Disney's death in 1966, the company continued expanding.

## Walt Disney World

Opened in Florida in 1971 and became one of the largest entertainment resorts in the world.

### Growth of Disney Parks

| Park                   | Opening Year |
| ---------------------- | ------------ |
| Disneyland             | 1955         |
| Walt Disney World      | 1971         |
| Tokyo Disneyland       | 1983         |
| Disneyland Paris       | 1992         |
| Hong Kong Disneyland   | 2005         |
| Shanghai Disney Resort | 2016         |

---

# 🎥 The Disney Renaissance

The late 1980s and 1990s marked a revival of Disney animation.

## Famous Films

* The Little Mermaid (1989)
* Beauty and the Beast (1991)
* Aladdin (1992)
* The Lion King (1994)
* Mulan (1998)

These films introduced memorable songs, advanced animation techniques, and worldwide success.

---

# 📺 Disney+

In 2019, Disney launched its streaming platform:

## Disney+

Features content from:

* Disney Animation
* Pixar
* Marvel
* Star Wars
* National Geographic

The service quickly became one of the world's largest streaming platforms.
